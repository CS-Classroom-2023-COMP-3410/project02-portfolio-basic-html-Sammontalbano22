From reading this collection, a few authors have made it to the top of my list. The first being Jules Verne. I love the great scientific depth that he goes into in his works. My favorite books of his is 20,000 Leagues Under the Sea. Jules Verne is ultimately who inspired me to travel to France. My other favorite author is Charles Dickens. I have now read much of his works including his lesser known short stories. While in the Uk, I had the privelege of visiting his house and seeing the original manuscripts for many of his books including The Mystery of Edwin Drood, which was the final novel he was working on when he passed away.

One of my biggest goals in life is to complete this list, which I am about 30% of the way through doing!
